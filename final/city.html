Aubrey, TX

History

Aubrey, TX was officially founded 1867 by a Civil War veteran named Lemuel Noah Edwards. He helped the town grow by giving each of his ten children land in which they could build their homes on. By year 1920 Aubrey has increased in population to about 700 people and had gained about 30 new businesses. Aubrey, TX is located twelve miles northeast of Denton and forty miles north of Dallas, TX. This city has come to be known as the "horse capital" of Texas, since the climate attracted many horse ranchers to this area.
