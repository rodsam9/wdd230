Target Audience:

Hows the weather looking for this upcoming week? You enjoy going outdoors and enjoy nature. You'd like to know if you can go outside today and not have to worry about a thunderstorm happening or gettin rained on. This webpage will help those who would like to know what the weather will be like in the South-East Idaho area, so that you can plan accordingly to still have a fun week!

Personas:

Pablo is 23 years old and enjoys going on hikes on the mountains. He's a very fit and active guy. He always checks the weather to ensure that his hikes will be safe. He always avoids going on hikes if the day seems like it will rain or make it difficult for him to hike such great, steep mountains.

Cristina is a 58 year old grandmother that loves spending time with her grandchildren. There's nothing better to her than to spend time with her grandchildren. She watches over her 2 grandkids that are the ages of 5 and 8. She loves taking them to the park daily so that they can have a great time playing with their friends. Cristina always checks to see what the weather would be like, to see if she should go to the park or plan for other activities in the area.

Scenario 1:

Steven loves hunting and found out recently that he's off work this weekend and wants to go hunt some deer. He has his guns and gear all set up, now he just doesn't know where would be a great place to hunt. He enters this website and finds out that they have a map showing where hunters are competing to kill deer. He is highly interested and plants to compete in this tournament.

Scenario 2:

Jonathan wants to try out for the swimming team at his high school, but needs to practice. He goes to a community pool to practice laps. He always checks to see how the weather will be like so that he can ensure that his practice swims are safe outdoors. He for sure doesn't want to swim when there's a thunderstrom outside.

Scenario 3:

Bob and Billy are always trying out new places to eat. For this weekend they are planning to find a place that will serve the best burgers in town. They go on this website and find listings of the best, 5 star burger restarurants in town. They now have to try all the burger restaurants to see which is their favorite.

Scenario 4:

Chandler wants to go horseback riding but isn't sure if it's worth getting burnt from the sun again. He quickly checks this webpage to see if it's a good day to go out and if he should wear sunscreen.

Scenario 5:

Clarissa is feeling a bit bored today. She's always wanting to try new things but can never seem to find out what to try next. She finds on the site that they have a section that shows things to do in the area. She now finds a huge list of thigns she can try out and not be bored.
