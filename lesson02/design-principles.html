Design Principles

White Space and Clean Design

Instagram

Instagram.com

Instagram does a great job showing white space and clean design on there log in/sign up webpage. There is lots of "white" area in between elements on this webpage. They did a great job displaying simple icons and images. I think gives users an easy way to navigate through the website without having any confusing instructions.

Hick's Law

Ebay

Ebay.com

Ebay does a excellent job in applying Hick's law on their webpage. Here you can see that the user has the option to filter out the item to his/her desired outcome. This makes it easier for users to find what they are looking for.

Visual Hierarchy

Ford

Ford.com

Ford does a great job getting the users attention on this nice car with a blue button pretty much telling users to click on it and view this amazing car. They clearly did a good job in displaying a new model by adding 2022, and showing that they have the newest and best car available.
